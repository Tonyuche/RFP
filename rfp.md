# Request for Proposal (RFP)

## Deployment of Wireless Infrastructure for TuchTech Ltd
</div>

### Introduction
TuchTech Ltd. is seeking proposals from qualified vendors for the design, installation, configuration, and support of a modern wireless network infrastructure across our office premises. The objective is to implement a secure, high-performance wireless environment that supports current and future business needs, including mobility, scalability, and secure access to company resources.

Vendors responding to this RFP should demonstrate experience in enterprise wireless deployments, network security, and ongoing technical support.
