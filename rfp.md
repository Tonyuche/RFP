# Request for Proposal (RFP)

## Deployment of Wireless Infrastructure for TuchSolutions Ltd
</div>

### About Us
**TuchSolutions Ltd.** is a customer service solutions company dedicated to helping organizations enhance client satisfaction, streamline support operations, and deliver exceptional service experiences. Through innovative technology, skilled personnel, and customer-centric strategies, TuchSolutions Ltd. empowers businesses to build strong relationships with their clients and achieve long-term loyalty.

### Introduction
**TuchSolutions Ltd.** is seeking proposals from qualified vendors for the design, installation, configuration, and support of a modern wireless network infrastructure across our office premises. The objective is to implement a secure, high-performance wireless environment that supports current and future business needs, including mobility, scalability, and secure access to company resources.

Vendors responding to this RFP should demonstrate experience in enterprise wireless deployments, network security, and ongoing technical support.
### Project Overview
**TuchSolutions Ltd.** requires a comprehensive wireless solution capable of delivering reliable, high-speed connectivity throughout the office. The new wireless network must support Wi-Fi 6 or newer standards, incorporate robust security features, integrate seamlessly with the existing wired infrastructure, and provide centralized management and monitoring. 

The current infrastructure is aging and fails to meet the demands of modern cloud-based applications, video conferencing, and the increasing density of user devices.
